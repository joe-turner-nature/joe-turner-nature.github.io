About

I am a biodiversity and marine spatial ecology specialist with over a decade of experience across academia, government, and consultancy. My work spans coral reef mapping during my PhD, Arctic habitat risk assessments, biodiversity risk screening for global finance, and development of internationally recognised tools such as the Marine STAR metric.

I have advised Fortune 500 companies, financial institutions, governments, and global initiatives including TNFD and SBTN, translating complex ecological science into defensible and feasible action.
